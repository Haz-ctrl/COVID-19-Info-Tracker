COVID-19 App Home Page


Welcome to the COVID-19 Statistics App! Click on the links below or on the hamburger menu (3 lines) in the top left to access one of the pages. The Global, Countries and Maps pages all show COVID-19 statistical data, from total cases, deaths and recoveries alongside other information. Pages make use of Chart.js as well as Mapbox to create UI elements. The SIR page explains predictive models for viruses like corona. Get started by clicking on one of the links below to go to one of the pages. Hope you enjoy!
